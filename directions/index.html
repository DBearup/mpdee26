The venue

The conference will be take place in the Sir Bob Burgess Building (SBB) at the University of Leicester campus, Leicester, LE2 6BF. The link above will take you to Google Maps. If at the central campus, proceed along University Road to the junction with Welford Road and turn left. Cross Welford Road at the next pedestrian crossing, walk past Fremen's Kitchen and Bar, and turn right. SBB will now be up the steps in front of you.

Getting to Leicester

Leicester is in the heart of England with excellent communications by road, air and rail. In particular, Leicester is less than one hour of travel by coach from East Midlands Airport, about 1.5 hours by train from London Luton and Birmingham International Airport, and within 2.5 hours by train from Heathrow and London Stansted.

Accommodation

The closest hotel is The Belmont Hotel (about 10-15 minutes by walking from the conference site). Alternatively, there is a broad variety of hotels in the city centre within walking distance from the conference site (25-30 minutes).

About the city

Leicester is one of the oldest English cities with over a thousand years of history. Modern Leicester is a lively, multi-cultural city with the population of nearly 300,000. Leicester has a huge choice of pubs, clubs, restaurants, cinemas and theatres as well as excellent shopping - from one of Europe's oldest markets to new shopping malls. Leicester is the home to the National Space Centre. It also boasts one of the largest pieces of Roman architecture in Britain (at the Jewry Wall Museum), and the largest collection of German Expressionist art in the country. One recent highlight is the burial ground & museum of King Richard III that was discovered just a decade ago.
